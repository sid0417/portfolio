The Best Anime According to Siddharth Roy

My top 3 Anime

One Piece

The Pirates are looking for the greatest treasure the world has to offer called One Piece. The main protaganist Monkey D Luffy is also set on a journey to find the One Piece and become the King of the Pirates

Naruto

This is a story of the boy named Naruto who want to become the Hokage of Hidden leaf Village

Attack on Titans

A place where titans are real and eat people, the main protaganist Eren Yeager want to kill all the titans. In order to kill all the titans Eren became a titan.
